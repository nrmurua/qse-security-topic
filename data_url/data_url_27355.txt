As the PlanQK Platform provides the attribute inverseTypeName, also the QC Atlas should support it.
Further, it enables the visibility of related PlanQK algorithms via our QC Atlas UI.
Therefore, also adapt the UI, see UST-QuAntiL/qc-atlas-ui#138.
Example:
algorithmRelationType: {
id: “f418379f-296e-4b3c-b925-4f9073ba365c”
inverseTypeName: “is used by”
name: “uses”}
